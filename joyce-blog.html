JOYCE MEYOR - Never give up!

March 6 , 2020

Hope is a powerful force, the enemy goes after it with vengeance. Guard the hope in your heart. Hope keeps you from worrying,
empowers you to stay calm and enables you to believe you can face tomorrow. Be determined; be committed; be patient; be positive; and be diligent in every area of your life - and whatever you do, never give up!

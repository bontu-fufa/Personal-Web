TRAVOR NOAH - Born a crime

March 5, 2020

A memoir about Trevor Noah's early life in South Africa, with frequent asides describing the atmosphere during apartheid. Noah's mother is a black South African woman, and his father is a white German man. This was illegal in South Africa, as mixed marriages were not allowed and mixed-race children could be deemed evidence of a crime.After Trevor is born, he must live in a way that does not give away his mixed parentage, which makes life hard for his mother. A gifted mimic, Trevor learned to become “a chameleon,” using language to gain acceptance in school and on the streets. All this challenges becouse of his face color.
